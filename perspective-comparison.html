Factory Map Perspective Comparison

Choose the viewing angle that best matches your vision

Option A: Subtle Isometric View

Description

USA map with gentle perspective angle (~15-20°). South appears closer, north appears further away. Creates depth while keeping the country outline fully recognizable.

✅ Pros

  • Stunning visual impact - looks like AoE3/Civ5
  • Creates sense of depth and dimension
  • More immersive and game-like
  • Professional, polished appearance
  • Matches strategy game aesthetic perfectly

⚠️ Cons

  • More complex coordinate transformations
  • Slightly longer development time
  • Need to adjust all positioning logic
  • Northern states appear smaller

Option B: Standard Top-Down View

Description

Traditional flat map view from directly above. All states shown at equal scale. Isometric buildings and sprites placed on flat map.

✅ Pros

  • Simpler implementation (current approach)
  • Easier coordinate calculations
  • Familiar perspective to users
  • Faster to develop and iterate
  • All regions equally visible

⚠️ Cons

  • Less visually impressive
  • Doesn't match strategy game reference as closely
  • Less sense of depth and immersion
  • More "flat" feeling
Note: Both options will have the same features, animations, and functionality. The only difference is the viewing angle. Choose based on which visual style better matches your vision!
